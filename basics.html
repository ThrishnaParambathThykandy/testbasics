What is Software Testing?

Software Testing is the process of evaluating a software application to identify defects and ensure that it meets the specified requirements.

The main goal of testing is to ensure quality, reliability, and usability before the product is released to users.

Why is Testing Important?

  • Identifies defects early
  • Ensures requirements are met
  • Improves software quality
  • Prevents production failures

Objectives of Testing

  • Verify functionality works as expected
  • Validate business requirements
  • Reduce risks
  • Build confidence in the product
