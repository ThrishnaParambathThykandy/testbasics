Error vs Bug vs Defect vs Failure

  • Error: Human mistake during coding or design
  • Bug: Coding issue found by developers
  • Defect: Deviation from expected behavior found during testing
  • Failure: Application crash or incorrect behavior in production

Defect Life Cycle

  1. New
  2. Assigned
  3. Open
  4. Fixed
  5. Retest
  6. Verified
  7. Closed
  8. Rejected / Deferred / Duplicate

Defect Management

Defect management is the process of identifying, logging, tracking, prioritizing, and closing defects effectively.

Defect Triage

Defect triage is a meeting where defects are reviewed and prioritized based on severity, priority, and business impact.

Severity vs Priority

  • Severity: Impact of defect on the system
  • Priority: Urgency of fixing the defect

Severity & Priority Combinations

  • High Priority, High Severity: App crash on login
  • High Priority, Low Severity: Spelling mistake on homepage
  • Low Priority, High Severity: Rare crash in edge scenario
  • Low Priority, Low Severity: UI alignment issue
